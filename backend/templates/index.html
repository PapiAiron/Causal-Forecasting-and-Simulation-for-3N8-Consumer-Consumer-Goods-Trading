I. Introduction & Rationale

This project focuses on optimizing the beverage supply chain by moving beyond traditional forecasting methods. We integrate external causal factors like promotions, holidays, and events to create a more accurate demand forecast.

The core of the system is the combination of **Causal Forecasting** to predict demand and **Discrete-Event Simulation** to test different supply chain scenarios. This approach allows for proactive decision-making, helping to prevent stockouts and reduce excess inventory.
